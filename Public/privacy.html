QRTurbo.app

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Overview

QRTurbo.app is a free browser-based QR code generator. The service is designed so that the QR code content you enter stays on your device. QR codes are generated locally in your browser.

Data You Enter

You may enter URLs, text, WiFi details, contact details, email content, event details, location data, phone numbers, app links, or upload a logo image to create a QR code. This information is processed in your browser and is not intentionally uploaded to QRTurbo.app servers.

Generated QR codes may contain the information you entered. Anyone who can scan or view a QR code you create may be able to read that encoded information.

Tracking, Analytics, and Advertising

QRTurbo.app does not use advertising networks, analytics trackers, tracking pixels, or third-party tracking scripts. The app does not create user accounts and does not profile visitors.

Cookies and Local Storage

QRTurbo.app does not use cookies. The app may use your browser's local storage to remember interface preferences, such as selected language and light or dark theme. These preferences stay in your browser unless you clear site data.

Server Logs and Hosting

QRTurbo.app is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process basic technical request information needed to deliver and protect the site, such as IP address, request time, requested URL, browser user agent, performance data, and security metadata. QRTurbo.app does not use this information for advertising or visitor profiling.

Third-Party Services

QRTurbo.app uses Cloudflare Pages for hosting and delivery. QR generation runs client-side, and the QR content you enter is not intentionally sent to Cloudflare by QRTurbo.app's application code. If you open external links from the site, such as the source code repository, those external services are governed by their own privacy practices.

Children's Privacy

QRTurbo.app is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. The service does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when the service changes. The updated date at the top of the page shows when the policy was last revised.
